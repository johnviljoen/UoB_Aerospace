          • Design Morphology and Preliminary Integration

            Design Morphology and Preliminary Integration
            Attached Files:
            •  AENG10001_Y1718_fixedwing_introduction-1.ppt AENG10001_Y1718_fixedwing_introduction-1.ppt - Alternative Formats (34.105 MB)
            •  AENG10001_Y1718_morphintegr_RED.ppt AENG10001_Y1718_morphintegr_RED.ppt - Alternative Formats (13.427 MB)
            •  AENG10001_Y1718_morphintegr.ppt AENG10001_Y1718_morphintegr.ppt - Alternative Formats (13.925 MB)

            You will find 3 sets of notes:

            1. The 1st is related to the Introduction of general fixed wing configurations (lecture of the 12/10/17)
            2. The second concerns the morphology details and basic design criteria for fixed wing configurations (lecture of the 17/10/17). The name of the related file is AENG10001_Y1718_morphintegr_RED. The contents of this file will be relevant to the exam.
            3. The third is an extended version of the previous document, with more details about the parametric design of fixed wing configurations. I invite you to read this document, because it provides quite a lot of introductory information related to parametric aircraft design that will be covered in future modules of the programme.
